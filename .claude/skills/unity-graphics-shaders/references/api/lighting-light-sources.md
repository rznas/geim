Resources for adding light to a **scene**, with Light components, **ambient light**, and cookies.

| **Page** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |

| Add ambient light from the environment | Improve the look of a scene with light that doesn’t come from a specific source. |
| Cookies | Resources for adding masks to lights to simulate caustics, soft shadows, and light shapes such as light entering from a window. |
