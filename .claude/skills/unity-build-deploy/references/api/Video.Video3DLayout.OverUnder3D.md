### Description

Video contains 3D content where the left eye occupies the upper half and right eye occupies the lower half of video frames.

Use this setting for VR and 3D videos that use the over-under format. VR and 3D videos need different eye perspectives to create depth. Useful for work with VR headsets, 3D TVs, and 360 degree videos.
