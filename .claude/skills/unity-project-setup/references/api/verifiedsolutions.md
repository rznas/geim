# Introduction to Verified Solutions

The [Verified Solutions program](https://assetstore.unity.com/verified-solutions) is a curated library of high-quality third-party assets and solutions. Unity evaluates and verifies these offerings to ensure they enhance core Unity products and maintain compatibility with commonly used Unity versions.

The Verified Solutions team evaluates your solution in the following ways:

- Evaluates solutions for quality and scalability to meet enterprise needs.
- Provides technical review and feedback from Unity’s Release QA team.
- Ensures compatibility with the most commonly used versions of Unity.

Once you become a Verified Solution, you have the following benefits:

- A Verified Solution badge on **Asset Store** pages, publisher websites, and marketing materials.
- Included in special collections with prioritized Asset Store listings.
- Featured in Asset Store marketing and promotional campaigns.
- A dedicated support line for onboarding help.
- Privileges in the Asset Store, including customized End User License Agreements (EULAs)
- Flexible payment models:
- **Subscription-based**: Pay a recurring price at regular intervals for ongoing access.
- **Consumption-based**: Pay based on actual resource usage (pay-per-use model).

**Important:** As a Verified Solution provider, you’re responsible for maintaining compatibility when Unity updates and addressing bugs promptly.

## Getting listed on the Verified Solutions page

The Verified Solutions team adds all Verified Solutions to the [business](https://unity.com/partners/verified-solutions) and [creator pages](https://unity.com/partners/verified-solutions-for-creators) on the Asset Store website ([assetstore.unity.com](https://assetstore.unity.com)).

You must submit the following information to the Verified Solutions team before your solution is added to the Unity.com website:

- A logo of the company or solution as an .EPS or .AI file, and a white version on a transparent background for the black background
- A product image with a horizontal layout in 16:9 aspect ratio
- Text that describes the company or solution in 50 words or fewer (30–40 words is preferred)

To provide this information, fill out the [Verified Solutions Technical Evaluation Survey](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScK1nxhg7C4753Ab4bJJON-2ZmmvYecg4uQapI3DXgyoCfTtw/viewform).

## Support for Verified Solutions

For general or technical support, or questions about the Verified Solutions program, contact the Unity Verified Solutions team at vs-support@unity3d.com.
